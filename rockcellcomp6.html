Choose Rockwool if you need:

  • Superior fire protection (fireproof buildings, wildfire zones).
  • Better moisture resistance (basements, humid climates).
  • Long-lasting insulation (50+ years with no settling).
  • Excellent soundproofing (ideal for multi-family homes, offices).

Choose Cellulose if you need:

  • A budget-friendly, eco-friendly option (lower carbon footprint).
  • Recycled material-based insulation for sustainable construction.
  • A viable DIY option (blown-in cellulose is easier for attic retrofits).

Overall Winner: Rockwool for Performance, Cellulose for Sustainability

While cellulose is cheaper and greener, Rockwool provides superior fire safety, durability, moisture resistance, and soundproofing, making it a better long-term investment.
