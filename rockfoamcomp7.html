Choose Rockwool if you need:

  • Fireproof insulation (homes, high-rises, wildfire zones).
  • Non-toxic, eco-friendly insulation.
  • Durability & soundproofing.

Choose Spray Foam or XPS if you need:

  • High R-value per inch for tight spaces.
  • Seamless air sealing (especially for attics & basements).
  • Waterproofing (closed-cell spray foam is best for flood-prone areas).

Overall Winner: Rockwool for Safety & Sustainability, Spray Foam for Air Sealing

Rockwool outperforms foam in fire resistance, soundproofing, sustainability, and long-term durability, while foam excels in higher R-values and air sealing.
