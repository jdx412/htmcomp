Choose Rockwool if you need:

  • Fireproof insulation (ideal for wildfire zones and fire-rated buildings).
  • Superior moisture resistance (best for basements, humid climates).
  • Long-lasting insulation (50+ years without sagging).
  • High soundproofing performance (perfect for multi-family homes and offices).

Choose Wood Fiber if you need:

  • A fully biodegradable and natural insulation.
  • Lower-carbon footprint insulation.
  • A breathable insulation material for vapor-open wall systems.

 

Overall Winner: Rockwool for Performance, Wood Fiber for Sustainability

While wood fiber insulation is greener, Rockwool provides better fire resistance, durability, and moisture control, making it a superior choice for safety and longevity.
