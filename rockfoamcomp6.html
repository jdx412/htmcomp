Choose Loose Rockwool if you need:

  • An environmentally friendlier option with fewer health risks and natural materials.
  • Insulation that offers excellent fire resistance and moisture resistance.

Choose Spray Foam if you need:

  • The best thermal performance and air sealing to maximize energy efficiency.
  • Insulation that maintains performance and structural integrity over time.

Overall Winner: Consider Your Needs

The choice between Loose Rockwool and Spray Foam depends largely on the specific requirements of your project. If environmental impact and ease of installation are your priorities, along with excellent fire and moisture resistance, Loose Rockwool is the preferable choice. However, if you're looking for the highest energy efficiency and the best air sealing properties, Spray Foam is the better option, especially in environments that demand the highest R-values and durability from insulation solutions. Each material has its strengths, making them suitable for different applications based on performance needs and environmental considerations.
