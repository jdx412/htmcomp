Performance Comparison: Loose Rockwool vs. Loose Cellulose Insulation

 

Both Loose Rockwool and Loose Cellulose are popular insulation choices, but they differ significantly in fire resistance, moisture resistance, durability, soundproofing, and sustainability. Here's a head-to-head breakdown:
